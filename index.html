Our Story

Born From the
Fire & the Sea

Ember & Salt was born from a simple belief — that Caribbean cuisine deserves the same reverence as any fine dining tradition. Chef Marcus James returned from training in Barbados and London with one goal: bring world-class technique home to Antigua.


Every recipe honors the island — scotch bonnet heat, fresh coconut, slow-braised meats, and the ocean's daily gift. We are not fusion. We are Caribbean, elevated.
